Game Rules

1.Objective:Be the first player to form a line of three of your marks (either "X" or "O") horizontally, vertically, or diagonally on the game board.

2.Gameplay: - Two players take turns to make their moves in empty cells. - Player 1 ("X") goes first, followed by Player 2 ("O"). - Players take turns placing their mark in an empty cell of the grid.

3.Winning Conditions: - A player wins if they have three of their marks in a row in any direction: horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

4.Ending the Game: - The game ends when: - One player forms a winning line. - All 9 cells on the grid are filled, resulting in a draw.

5.Draw (Tie) Condition: - If all cells are filled, and no player has won, the game is a draw (tie).
